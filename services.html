Our Comprehensive Range Of Services

1. Land Freight (Trucking Services)

• We operate a modern and diverse fleet equipped for safe, reliable, and well-coordinated deliveries of all types of goods:


• Refrigerated Trucks: For perishable, food, and temperature-sensitive cargo.


• Flatbed and Lowbed Trailers: Ideal for heavy equipment, machinery, oversized loads, and containers.


• Box and Curtain Trailers: For general goods, high-value, or protected shipments.


• Small and Medium Trucks: For local deliveries and express transport needs.


• We are also enhancing our operations with GPS-enabled tracking systems to offer better visibility and coordination for our clients.

2. Destinations We Serve

We proudly cover major trade routes across the GCC and the wider Middle East, managing full customs clearance, documentation, and border coordination to ensure smooth, timely deliveries.


Our current destinations include:


• UAE: All Emirates — Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, RAK, UAQ, Fujairah.


• Saudi Arabia: Riyadh, Dammam, Jeddah, Makkah, Madinah, Qassim, Tabuk, Jizan, Abha, and more.


• Kuwait, Qatar, Oman, Bahrain — with regular and reliable routes.


(With ongoing expansion plans to further strengthen our regional network.)
