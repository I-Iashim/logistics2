About HBA Logistic

Logistic Simplified!

Your Preferred Logistic & Freight Partner

Introduction

At HBA Logistics, we deliver more than cargo — we deliver trust, precision, and excellence.

Headquartered in Dubai, UAE, we are a growing logistics provider offering comprehensive land, sea, and air freight solutions across the GCC and beyond.

With a reliable network, skilled professionals, and modern coordination systems, we ensure every shipment is handled with care, transparency, and efficiency.

Who We Are

HBA Logistic Services LLC is a leading freight and logistics company based in Dubai, UAE.

We are powered by professionals with decades of expertise in global freight and logistics solutions.

Our services are tailor-made and we go the extra mile to exceed client expectations.

With a strong network spanning 120+ countries, we ensure simplified, cost-effective, and reliable transportation.

We take pride in being the preferred logistics partner for long-term, trusted global supply chain solutions.
